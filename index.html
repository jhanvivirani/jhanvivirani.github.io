Some Things I Do

Data Analyst

I work at the InfoSeeking Lab at the Rutgers School of Communication and Information. I'm currently working on a project in collaboration with the United Nations called Armed Conflicts Analytics, which is a data visualization platform for the United Nations.

Newspaper Columnist

I write a column for the Daily Targum newspaper. My column, "From Breaks to Bars" analyzes hip-hop news and explores the nuances of hip-hop history and culture.

Research Assistant

Tutor
