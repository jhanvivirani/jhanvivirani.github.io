Current Projects

Data Analyst and Manager

I work at the InfoSeeking Lab at the Rutgers School of Communication and Information. I work on developing and managing research projects as a part of an open collaboration between universities like Rutgers and the United Nations. One such project is the Armed Conflicts Analytics Project , which is a data visualization and analysis platform which centers around data dealing with the dynamics of war and armed conflict around the world.

President of Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society

Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society is an on-campus student organization that connects students to opportunities in the start-up business field. In the past year we've grown to be one of the most influential clubs on campus. We host speaker series, student workshops, and collaborative events to help students develop as thinkers and leaders. My work includes overseeing launching of new programs and facilitating projects between board members.

Co-Founder and Director at RamenxRES

RamenxRES is the first on-campus start-up incubator at Rutgers University- New Brunswick. Specifically designed for Rutgers students, this twelve-week program takes start-ups through the preliminary necessities of building a business, from market research to product development to business model creation. My work includes all big-vision curriculum planning to working with student organizations, experts, and vc sponsors to facilitate partnerships between the start-ups and potential advisors.

Student Government Representative

I am the Busch Caucus Chair for the Rutgers University Student Association, in which I try my best to create positive impact for students in my community.

Research Assistant

I am a research assistant at the Rutgers School of Management, where I help conduct research on disabilities in the workforce.
